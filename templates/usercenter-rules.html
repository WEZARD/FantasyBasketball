Fantasy Basketball Manager

  • RULES
Choose Battle Field

Everyday the system will open some normal battle fileds, each battle field get its own rewards, which includes diamonds or virtual coins,etc. Users can choose the battle field depending on what rewards he wants.

Player Worth

Depends on each NBA player's stas of the season and stats of recent games, the system will calculate each player's worth. The total worth of players you choose can't exceed the limit.

Build Your Own tTam

you can build your own 'dream-team' from all players who have game to play that day. The five players you choose have to fill 5 position as one point guard, one score guard, one small foward, one power foward and one center. Each NBA player has his own postion, for example, Chris Paul, he is a point guard. For some of the players, they got 2 positions to be chosen, such as Paul Gasol, he can play as a power forward or a center.

You can submit your team after you have chosen 5 players for you.

The submit time must be before the deadline of the battle field you chose to fight in.

Change Your Team

You can change the players in your team before the deadline of the battle field.

Ranking Rules

Depending on each NBA player's actual performance(stats), the system will calculate each player's fantasy score by the unique formula.

The total score of your team will be the sum of the five player's own fantasy score. After all the games of each day end, the system will get the ranking of all the users in the each battle field base on the total score.

If two users share the same total score, the team which has the less worth of players will win. If they still share the same worth of players, the one who submit his team earlier will win.

Special Occasion

For any reason, if the player you chose did not show on the games, the fantasy score of this player will be zero.
